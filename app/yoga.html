Yoga is a healing modality. Mentally. Physically. Emotionally. Personally, it has helped me immensely in overcoming stress, injury & anxiety. I enrolled in my first teacher-training because deep down I saw how much the practice of yoga healed me. I felt compelled to bring that same healing & peace to everyone else.

What I offer:

1 on 1 Sessions - create the type of class that YOU want. The time and space is all yours!

Group Sessions (up to 8) - move and breathe with family or friends.

Events/Workshops - hosting an event? Offer a yoga + meditation session to set the tone.

Certifications: 200hr ERYT in Power, Sculpt, Yin + meditation with the addition of yoga/barre classes stemming from my ballet background.
